About Us

What is it all about?

The Design My Tee Competition is a freestyle design competition open to both concept stage and realized works designed by professional and young fashion designers, tailors, stylists, fashion design companies and other business in this industry worldwide.


How to participate in a competition?

This gives you chance to create your own design on a t-shirt and compete with other designers in a variety of competitions. ​The competitions are held and uploaded regularly and last a certain number of days.For more details on these competitions please refer to the competitions tab. ​
Designers who want to be a part of the competition can upload their t-shirt design within this window, after which submissions are closed. ​All logged-in users can vote on the designs and the design with the highest number of votes wins. ​All the people who voted are automatically added into a lucky draw and someone is randomly selected to be sent a copy of the printed t-shirt with the winning competition design. ​
